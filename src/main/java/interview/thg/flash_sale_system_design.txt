Design an order management system capable of handling a 100x traffic spike during a limited-time flash sale.
Customers will attempt to purchase limited-stock products simultaneously. The system must process orders reliably while maintaining inventory accuracy and providing a good customer experience.
Requirements
Accept a large volume of concurrent purchase requests.
Prevent overselling of inventory.
Process orders and payments reliably.
Handle duplicate requests (e.g., users clicking "Buy" multiple times).
Make order events available to downstream systems such as fulfillment, notifications, and analytics.
Consider
High-level infrastructure and service design.
Scalability and traffic spike handling.
Fault tolerance and recovery mechanisms.
Database bottlenecks and storage strategy.
Caching requirements.
Rate limiting and backpressure.
Consistency vs availability trade-offs.
You may make reasonable assumptions regarding traffic volume, inventory size, latency requirements, and SLAs.
